Studiyo combines Beauty, Quality and Simplicity to create an emotional experience. You can play music by talking talking directly to the speaker. Say "Play my morning mix" or "Play Drake". you don't need your phone, I've heard the sound that comes out of it and it sounds really good.


So what makes the speaker different from th 458 other speaker out there? it starts with the design. the bold, pill-shape design will stand out in your living room.

The Studiyo speaker connects over WI-FI or bluetooth, works with AirPlay and Spotify Connect and can stream from many different music streaming services out of the box. Studiyo is compatible with Spotify, Apple music, soundCloud, Tidal, Deezer and Google Play Music.


Our elegant touch interface lets you control your music directly from the speaker. Tap on the glass touch panel to play a song, swipe to go next, zoom with two fingers to increase volume and more. it's the most intuitive interface ever on a speaker. Control your music anywhere in the room, without a phone.

Ask for a track, Artiste, Playlist or Radio. Say it, play it. Its that easy.

NO. 01

Aesthetic


We Design Studiyo not only to be beautiful and pure, but to also have a touch of human presence.

To do that we elevated Studiyo vertically and enclosed its powerful sound with an iconic three dimensionally knitted envelop that integrates harmoniously into everyone's home.

NO. 02

High Quality

We've been doing our best work for the past two years to create a speaker that matches our standard of excellence. We've sourced among the best materials around the planet to craft a high-quality and object.

Developed on Grand-Rapids, Michigan, Studiyo's soft cloth is the result of years of U.S. knitting savoir-faire and craftsmanship. It perfectly allows sound to spread. it gives Studiyo a both a soft touch and beautiful look.
